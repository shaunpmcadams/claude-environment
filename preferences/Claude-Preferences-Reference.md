## What Preferences Are

Standing instructions in settings about tone, formatting, feature usage. Injected as "user preferences" for every non-incognito conversation.

## Preferences vs. Related Systems

| System | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| **Preferences** | "Always do X" behavioral rules |
| **Memory Edits** | Factual corrections and information |
| **Styles** | Writing tone and structure |
| **Project Instructions** | Project-specific behavior |
